## ROTATION

### Speed Farming (With Argent Veil)
```
1. Falling Star into pack
2. Blessed Hammer x3
3. EVADE CONSTANTLY (spawns more hammers)
4. Move to next pack
5. Keep Auras active
```

### Pit Pushing
```
1. Falling Star -> Arbiter Form
2. Condemn -> Group + Vulnerable
3. Blessed Hammer spam
4. Evade for repositioning
5. Rally if needed
```

## PARAGON BOARDS

### Board Order

| # | Board | Glyph | Legendary Node |
|---|-------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | Starter | Spirit | (glyph socket only) |
| 2 | **CASTLE** | **Exploit** | **Castle - 1.8% Armor = Damage** |
| 3 | Relentless | Challenger | Dervish - CDR while moving |
| 4 | Shield Bearer | Sentinel | Shield Bearer - Block doubles damage |
| 5 | Beacon | Resplendence | Resplendent - Aura casts reset skills |

**PRIORITY:** Rush Castle board FIRST. Herald of Zakarum doubles your armor, Castle converts armor to damage.

### Glyph Leveling (Level to 15 first, then 21)
1. **Exploit** - Vulnerable damage (Castle board)
2. **Challenger** - Damage while moving (Relentless board)
3. Spirit - Core skill damage (Starter board)
4. Sentinel - DR when blocking (Shield Bearer board)
5. Resplendence - Healing effectiveness (Beacon board)
