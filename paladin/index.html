Armor = Damage (Castle Math)

How Castle Paragon Works

Node Effect1.8% of your Armor becomes damage
10,000 Armor= 180% increased damage
13,500 Armor= 243% increased damage
20,000 Armor= 360% increased damage

This is why armor is both offensive AND defensive for this build.

Herald of Zakarum Shield Math

The tooltip lies. It says "+50% [x] Armor" but it's actually additive, not multiplicative.

What tooltip saysx1.5 multiplier
What it actually does+50% added to armor pool
Real benefit+50% Strength, +Core Skill ranks

Still BiS because of the Strength and skill ranks, but don't expect armor to literally double.

Resistance System (S11)

Diminishing Returns Formula

Max Possible DR90%Theoretical limit (never reachable)

Invocation RuneTriggers effect when enough Offering accumulated
Socket LocationHelm, Chest, Pants, or 2H Weapon (need 2 sockets)
Max Runewords2 active at once

Endgame Runewords (Priority Order)

1. Cir + Gar (CRIT CHANCE)

Cir (Ritual)Generates offering on specific triggers
Gar (Invocation)Up to 25% Critical Strike Chance

Best for: High attack speed builds (this build). Stacks crit quickly. Essential for hitting 100% crit chance.

Gar is easy to find early. This is your budget-to-BiS runeword.

2. Cem + Ohm (EVADE SYNERGY)

Cem (Ritual)Generates offering on Evade
Ohm (Invocation)War Cry buff (+15% damage, +Move Speed)

Best for: This build! You're evading constantly → permanent War Cry uptime.

3. Moni + Ceh (VULNERABLE)

EffectHandles Vulnerable uptime automatically

Frees you from relying on Condemn for Vulnerable. Good if you want to drop Condemn for another skill.

4. Tam + Ohm (CORE SKILL SPAM)

Tam (Ritual)Cast Core Skill → 25 Offering
Ohm (Invocation)600 Offering → War Cry

Alternative to Cem+Ohm. Better if you manually cast Blessed Hammer often.

5. Bac + Lac (DEFENSE)

Bac (Ritual)Travel 5 meters → 50 Offering
Lac (Invocation)400 Offering → Challenging Shout (DR)

Permanent DR while moving. Good for Pit pushing.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Common Problems

No Faith (early game)Use Advance between Hammer casts. 2 Advance : 1 Hammer ratio.
Can't hit anythingGet in MELEE range. Advance INTO enemies.
Arbiter keeps droppingStack more CDR. Temper Arbiter Duration on amulet.
Low damageRush Castle paragon board. Check armor stacking.
Dying to Rally HP costKeep Defiance Aura ON. Get High Charity passives (30% healing).
Evade on cooldownNeed boots with "Attacks Reduce Evade Cooldown" affix.
Build feels slowGet Argent Veil ring. Without it, you have to manually cast hammers.
