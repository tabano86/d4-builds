```
[Core]   Blessed Hammer <- Damage
[1]      Defiance Aura  <- Keep active
[2]      Rally          <- When Faith empty (after lvl 12)
[3]      (empty)
[4]      (empty)
```

---

## HOW TO PLAY (SIMPLE)

```
1. Run up to enemies
2. ADVANCE 2-3 times (builds Faith)
3. Blessed Hammer 1 time
4. ADVANCE 2-3 times (refill Faith)
5. Blessed Hammer 1 time
6. Repeat

If Faith empty: Rally -> Advance spam
```

### THE GOLDEN RULE
**2 Advance hits for every 1 Hammer**

---

## GEAR PRIORITY

Just equip whatever has:
1. Higher weapon damage (MOST IMPORTANT)
2. +Blessed Hammer if you see it
3. Life/Armor for survival

Don't overthink it yet.

---

## GEMS

Put whatever gems you find in sockets. Don't waste gold upgrading.

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Only using Hammer | USE ADVANCE to generate Faith |
| Fighting at range | Get in MELEE range |
| Ignoring Defiance Aura | Turn it ON and leave it on |
